Privacy Policy for SolidNote

Last Updated: May 24, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to SolidNote, an application provided by Eastern Labs ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application, SolidNote (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our service to you. This includes: User Content (projects, issues, photos you create), Account Information (email address if you subscribe), and automatically collected data like Purchase History and anonymous Diagnostics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide the App's core functionality, manage your subscription, improve the App by fixing bugs, and communicate with you. For the initial version of SolidNote, all of your project and issue data is stored locally on your device; we do not have access to it.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our app, such as Apple (for In-App Purchases) and RevenueCat (to manage subscription status), under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Choices

You can manage the information you provide by editing or deleting your projects and issues directly within the App. You can manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@easternlabs.app.
