Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Information We Collect

DreamLock collects minimal data necessary to provide sleep tracking and app blocking services. This includes:

  • Sleep session data (duration, timestamps)
  • App blocking preferences
  • Subscription status (managed by RevenueCat)

All data is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults with App Groups.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Track sleep sessions and calculate rewards
  • Manage app blocking preferences
  • Provide subscription services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data Storage

All sleep tracking and user preference data is stored locally on your device. We use Apple's Screen Time API for app blocking, which operates entirely on-device. Subscription data is managed by RevenueCat in accordance with their privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

DreamLock uses:

  • RevenueCat for subscription management
  • Apple Screen Time API for app blocking

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Manage subscriptions through your Apple ID settings
  • Request information about data practices

Children's Privacy

DreamLock is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app and the "Last Updated" date will be revised.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Privacy Policy for SolidNote

Last updated: July 2025

Introduction

SolidNote ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Project and issue data you create within the app
  • Photos you take and store within the app
  • Account information (if you choose to create an account)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data

Subscription Information

  • Payment information (processed securely through Apple/Google)
  • Subscription status and renewal dates

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the SolidNote service
  • Process your subscription payments
  • Improve our app functionality and user experience
  • Send you important updates about the app
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Your project data and photos are stored locally on your device
  • We do not have access to your local data unless you explicitly share it

Cloud Storage (Coming Soon)

  • When cloud sync is implemented, your data will be encrypted and stored securely
  • You will have control over what data is synced

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat

  • We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions
  • RevenueCat processes payment information according to their privacy policy
  • No personal data is shared with RevenueCat beyond what's necessary for subscription management

Apple/Google

  • Payment processing is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • These platforms have their own privacy policies governing payment data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection (where applicable)
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

Children's Privacy

SolidNote is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@easternlabs.app
Website: https://easternlabs.app

Data Retention

  • Local data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Subscription data is retained as required by law and for billing purposes
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time

International Users

If you are using SolidNote from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located.
